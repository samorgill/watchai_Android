Android version of Watchai

Using the Watchai Android app
To see the things registered to your account click “Things” from the Home screen. All your things will load all things currently registered to your account, they will display as either on or off and clicking on them will alter the state of the thing. 
Recipes contain mixes of functions.
Sleep will play soft music and lights
Smooth will play soft music and soothing lights
Wake will open the curtains and play music
Entertain will play entertaining music and lively lights
Emergency will flash a red light
Music contains 4 types a music that can be played and stopped from the watch
Monitor allows users to:
Monitor light levels 
Monitor movement
Monitor the temperature
To log out of your account click “log out”
To register your device with your Watchai Hub click register.
